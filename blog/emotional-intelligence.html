What is Emotional Intelligence?

If emotions are like tennis balls, then emotional intelligence is if you could catch and control what you do with them, instead of getting smacked in the face.

What is Emotional Intelligence?

It's a learnt ability using your neuroplasticity to learn the different emotions, in yourself and others, see things coming, understand them, and manage them effectively.

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ is cognitive while EQ is emotional.

Why is EQ important?

In both everyday life and work-life it helps with,

  • Having nicer healthier relationships around you,
  • Better more effective communication,
  • Self-regulation and comfort,
  • Creating stronger motivation,
  • Being a better leader or team member,
  • Generally being a better, nicer person.
