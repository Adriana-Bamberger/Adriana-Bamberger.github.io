How understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people

It can boost confidence in your ability to learn and grow.
It will Increase motivation, When we see that our brains can change and grow in response to our habits and actions, we are more willing to put in the hard work and hours.
It also helps you view challenges as opportunities and chances for growth and new skills rather than too hard obstacles!

How to engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit

  • Practice saying "Yet" instead of "can't"
  • The more we repeat a new skill, habit or behavior, the stronger the neural pathways become.
  • Getting more sleep so that my brain actually has time to consolidate the memories and neural pathways will also help.

Growth mindset

What is it and why is it relevant?

It keeps you motivated to keep growing, to become a better person, to do more good.
It keeps stress, anxiety, and depression at bay.
In general people with a growth mindset believe they can improve in any area with effort.

In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?

Not really, this is all things my parents have been drilling into me since I was old enough to understand.
I'm starting to see this may be different for other people. It doesn't look like this was talked enough about in kiwi primary, and intermediate schools.
