Te Whare Tapa Whā - My Wellbeing Plan

Core - Sprint 4 - 8/4/2024 - Dev Academy Foundations

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Going to lightsaber dueling and flow classes every Wednesday evening.
Walking to get public transport everywhere as I don't drive.

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

Eating dinner and enjoying some entertainment together with my home family most nights
Having family dinner at my parents place at least once every two weeks

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

Doing a quick follow along meditation in the mornings
Reading a chapter or a short part of something fiction and something non-fiction from my reading list most nights before bed.

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

Digital hand written journaling most nights before bed.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Walking to buses and trains, I wear barefoot shoes for medical reasons, this helps me feel "grounded" too.
Working with my windows open for fresh air.
Whenever I'm at my parents house once every week or so it's rural, amongst grass, bush etc. I'm always roped into helping do or fix something outside.
